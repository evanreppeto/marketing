An AI marketing operator you actually open every morning.

Not a dashboard with an AI bolted on — Arc is the interface. You open it to see what needs you, talk to the agent to get work drafted, and approve what ships. Calm, fast, and evidence-backed, with the restraint of Linear and the conversational ease of Claude. These are concept renders of that direction — a north star, not pixel specs.

01

One home: “what needs me?”

Open the app and in five seconds see waiting approvals, fresh opportunities, and what’s live. The daily loop, not a feature museum.

02

The agent is the interface

A Claude-grade conversation that can read, draft, and act across the whole product. Chat is the fast path; the pages are the audit trail.

03

Approval-native, never autonomous

Nothing reaches the outside world without a human. “Outbound stays locked” is on every surface. Trust is the product.

04

Evidence on everything

Opportunities cite sources, assets carry provenance, recommendations show confidence. No black-box AI.

05

Restraint as luxury

Warm obsidian, gold used once per screen, hairlines not card-soup, one editorial serif moment. Premium is what you leave out.

06

Multi-tenant & fast

Workspace switcher, connectors, team roles, ⌘K everywhere, instant-save. A real SaaS, configurable per business — not hardcoded.
